About Me

My name is Buse Aydöre (pronounced like "boo-say adore"). I’ve worn many hats in my background--from Fashion Designer to Sales Stylist and even Entrepreneur. Throughout my career, I've seen the powerful results that come with practicing a user-centered approach, so when I discovered there's a field dedicated to User Experience, it felt like the appropriate next step. I embarked on this new journey in 2020 to expand my skillset and reach an exciting industry. Primarily, my interests lie on the User Interface side (wireframing, prototyping, and animating). While I am a perpetual work-in-progress, my goal is to bring users simple, inclusive, and delightful experiences.

In my free time, I like staying up to date on the latest multidisciplinary trends. I also enjoy practing daily yoga, experimenting in the kitchen, and hosting game nights.
